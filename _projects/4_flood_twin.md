Kerala's recurring floods are a high-impact, data-rich testbed for the continuum ideas above. SUTRA is building an **LLM-orchestrated** flood-management system and a **digital twin of Kochi**: an LLM planner decides which model to run at which fidelity, on which compute tier, and when to escalate — an orchestration problem over sensors, simulations, and edge/cloud resources.

It connects our smart-city and environmental analytics work — sustainable federated learning for air-quality prediction {% cite dey2026greenedge %} and predictive smog analytics for health risk {% cite dey2026psychair %} — with the continuum-orchestration engine. The research core is decision-making under uncertainty, not systems integration.

**Open directions:** LLM planning under resource constraints, digital-twin fidelity management, and trustworthy societal-scale deployment.
